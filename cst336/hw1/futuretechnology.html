What's in store for Computer Graphics?

Computer Graphics although being prominent in our everyday life is still a growing field. With the creation of Artificial Intelligence in the recent years the ceiling for graphics technology has heavily been raised

Here are some examples of the technologies currently being researched:

Technology Description Impact
AI-Assisted Rendering Utilization of neural networks allowing for upscale and predictive visual details. Improves and reduces rendering times and hardware costs.
Generative Graphics Combining AI and 3D models to generate scenes and textures seamlessly. Revolutionizing content creation in films, gaming, and production.
Humanoid Robotics Utilizating Nvidia NUM microservices to develop simulations in order to train humanoid robots. Increase Productivity and reducing human demonstration data inputs.
